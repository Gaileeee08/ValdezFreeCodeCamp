In Loving Memory of my old self

Justine Gail Valdez

Januaray 30, 2000

Sometimes, there are dreams and aspirations you wish you could have pursued, but I understand how difficult it can be to fight for what you love when you come from a dysfunctional family. The impact of that environment often makes it hard to function and thrive. You may look back and think about all the things you could have done better if not for those struggles.
